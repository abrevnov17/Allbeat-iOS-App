- BrowseViewController
	- Can display posts and user profiles
- BrowseViewControllerDataSource
	- Inherets from AudioDataSource
	- nextCell(index: Int, callback: BrowseViewControllerDataSourceCallback) -> BrowseCell

- AudioDataSource
	- Used for background audio playback
	- nextAudioPiece(index: Int, callback: AudioDataSourceCallback) -> Audio
- AudioPlayer
	- playing: Bool {get set}
	- next()
	- previous()
	- stop()
- AudioPlayerDelegate
	- Used to determine player change
	- startedPlayingAudio(audioPlayer: AudioPlayer, audio: Audio)
	- timeChanged(audioPlayer: AudioPlayer, time: Float)
	- paused(audioPlayer: AudioPlayer)
	- played(audioPlayer: AudioPlayer)
